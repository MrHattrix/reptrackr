Privacy Policy for RepTrackr

Last Updated: July 2026

Overview

RepTrackr is a workout tracking application designed to operate primarily offline. Your privacy is important, and the app is built to collect as little information as possible.

Data Collection

RepTrackr does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

All workout data (including exercises, sets, reps, weights, routines, and statistics) is stored locally on your device.

Data Usage

Your data is used only to provide app functionality such as workout tracking, statistics, and progress history.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Android's internal storage and/or database systems. The developer does not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

The current version of RepTrackr does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services.

Data Security

While reasonable measures are taken to protect data, no system is completely secure. Users are responsible for maintaining backups of their device data.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app. Continued use of the app implies acceptance of any changes.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing.
